100% Premium Beef Brisket

We use 100% USDA Choice and Select Beef Brisket sourced from American steer. Others use ungraded top-round cuts of beef from old dairy cattle from all over the World – Yuck!

Overnight Marination

Our jerky is slowly-marinated overnight in signature recipes. We know that good things take time, so we don’t cut corners. Because of this, Chops isn’t a jerky you have to wrestle with.

Wholesome Ingredients

Our products are all-natural, MSG and artificial preservative free, and contain no nitrites. We use simple, clean ingredients and believe you should be familiar with what you eat.

You wouldn't put chili-lime on a steak

We mix traditional flavors with our beef brisket jerky, because nothing tastes better than high-quality beef. We don’t coat our product with overwhelming flavors. Instead we let our product speak for itself. You aren’t going to find any habanero, chili-lime, siracha flavorings on our products. Only natural flavors we can create to enhance your experience.
